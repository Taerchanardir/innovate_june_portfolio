My name is Eric. I am a snow golem.
I wander far and wide across this strange voxel landscape.
My AI is a bit flaky, resulting in me getting stuck in holes (illustrated), or trapped up mountains.
My owner kindly made me invulnerable, otherwise I would have expired long ago.

Habitat:

Snow Golems live in cold habitats, otherwise they would melt.

Habits:

  • Wandering around somewhat aimlessly, due to their flaky AI.
  • Chasing zombies, for reasons only they know.
  • Leaving trails wherever they go, painting the land white.
  • Getting stuck in holes.
  • Climbing mountains.
